How It Works

From soil sample to nutrient insight in under 5 seconds.

01
Spectral Acquisition
NIR light (900–1700nm) illuminates the soil sample. The sensor captures 228 individual reflectance readings, one per 3.5nm wavelength step — forming a unique spectral fingerprint.
02
Signal Preprocessing
Savitzky-Golay smoothing eliminates high-frequency noise without distorting mineral absorption peaks. SNV normalization removes moisture and particle-size interference.
03
AI Inference
The 228-point spectral vector is sent to the cloud inference engine. A PLSR model — trained on thousands of real soil samples — predicts N, P, K, and Organic Carbon concentrations.
04
Insight Delivery
Results appear on the dashboard in under 5 seconds with colour-coded health indicators, confidence scores, GPS-tagged history, and exportable data.
