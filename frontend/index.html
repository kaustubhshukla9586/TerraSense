Why TerraSense?

Traditional soil testing takes 5-7 days, costs thousands, and requires sending samples to distant labs. TerraSense replaces this entire process with a pocket-sized NIR sensor and AI — delivering laboratory-grade nutrient analysis in under 5 seconds, right in your field.
