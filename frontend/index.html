How It Works

From soil sample to nutrient insight in under 5 seconds.

01

Spectral Acquisition

NIR light (900–1700nm) illuminates the soil sample. The sensor captures 401 individual reflectance readings forming a unique spectral fingerprint of the soil's mineral composition.

02

Signal Preprocessing

Savitzky-Golay smoothing eliminates high-frequency noise. SNV normalization removes moisture and particle-size interference so only mineral content drives the prediction.

03

AI Inference

The 401-point spectral vector is sent to the cloud inference engine. A PLSR model trained on thousands of real soil samples predicts N, P, K, and OC concentrations.

04

Insight Delivery

Results appear on the dashboard in under 5 seconds with colour-coded health indicators, confidence scores, and full scan history for trend analysis.
